Ice Cream Cookie Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2¼ cups (280g) flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (230g) butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100g) white sugar
  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (175g) chocolate chips
  • 1 cup (160g) Candy-coated Chocolates Ice cream of your choice

Directions:

  • 1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C).
  • 2. In a bowl whisk flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
  • 3. In a large bowl cream the butter with sugars until light and fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla extract and stir until combined.
  • 4. stir in the flour mixture. stir just until incorporated. Divide into two equal portions. To first part add chocolate chips, to the second add candy-coated Chocolates and stir. This step is optional, you can use only chocolate chips, or only Candy-coated Chocolates. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  • 5. Roll balls of dough and place 2 inches (5cm) apart on the baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until very lightly browned on the sides. Let cool completely.
  • 6. Take a large scoop of ice cream and sandwich it between two cookies. Roll the sides with your favorite topping.
  • 7. Freeze until serving.

Wafer Ice Cream Delight

Ingredients:

  • 5 (1 oz each) pkg Hy-Vee peanut butter nutty wafers
  • 3 c. Hy-Vee vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1/2 c. Skippy creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tsp. Hy-Vee vanilla

Directions:

  • 1. Break nutty wafers in half lengthwise. Set the top halves aside.
  • 2. Line an 8-by-8-inch pan with the bottom halves of the nutty wafers.
  • 3. Combine the ice cream, peanut butter, and vanilla in a medium mixing bowl. Mix well.
  • 4. Spread the ice cream mixture evenly over the nutty wafers. Top with remaining nutty wafers.
  • 5. Cover and freeze at least 4 hours or overnight.
